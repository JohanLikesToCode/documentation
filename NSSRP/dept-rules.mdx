## Department Behavior & Operational Guidelines

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="General Rules">
    <Card title="All Departments">
      - Follow **chain of command** at all times.
      - Only use vehicles, equipment, and tools appropriate for your **rank**.
      - Abide by **roleplay realism standards** — no unrealistic actions or glitches.
      - Do not interfere with other department operations unless authorized.
      - Report **incidents, breaches, or emergencies** promptly via the proper channels.
    </Card>
    <Note>
      Repeated violations may result in suspension, rank demotion, or removal from the department.
    </Note>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Norfolk Southern Police Department">
    <AccordionGroup>
      <Accordion title="Rank Responsibilities" icon="shield">
        | Rank                | Responsibilities |
        | ------------------ | ---------------- |
        | Officer / Deputy    | Patrol assigned areas, respond to calls, follow orders from supervisors |
        | Sergeant / LT      | Supervise junior officers, approve high-priority actions, coordinate units |
        | Captain / Sheriff  | Oversee all department operations, authorize restricted vehicle use, manage personnel |
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="Operational Guidelines" icon="map-pin">
        - Always **use sirens and lights** when responding to high-priority calls.
        - Maintain **radio discipline**; avoid unnecessary transmissions.
        - **Backup protocols:** Request additional units when handling high-risk situations.
        - Engage only in **approved roleplay scenarios**; prohibited: gang RP, suicide RP, and unapproved large-scale crime scenes.
      </Accordion>
    </AccordionGroup>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Norfolk Southern Fire & Rescue">
    <AccordionGroup>
      <Accordion title="Rank Responsibilities" icon="fire">
        | Rank                | Responsibilities |
        | ------------------ | ---------------- |
        | Firefighter / EMT    | Respond to calls, operate apparatus, assist in rescues |
        | Lieutenant / Captain | Supervise crews, coordinate multi-unit incidents, ensure safety protocols |
        | Chief / Director     | Manage department resources, authorize special equipment, oversee large-scale operations |
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="Operational Guidelines" icon="map-pin">
        - Respond **promptly to all emergency calls** while observing safe driving practices.
        - Follow **chain of command** when on-scene.
        - Maintain and check all **equipment regularly**.
        - Coordinate with PD and DOT during **multi-department incidents**.
      </Accordion>
    </AccordionGroup>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Norfolk Department of Transportation">
    <AccordionGroup>
      <Accordion title="Rank Responsibilities" icon="truck">
        | Rank                 | Responsibilities |
        | ------------------- | ---------------- |
        | Operator / Driver     | Perform routine road duties, operate assigned vehicles |
        | Supervisor / Foreman  | Oversee crews, approve vehicle use, maintain road safety standards |
        | Director / Chief      | Plan operations, authorize restricted vehicles, manage department resources |
      </Accordion>
      <Accordion title="Operational Guidelines" icon="map-pin">
        - Ensure **roadway safety** at all times.
        - Perform **maintenance and inspections** on vehicles before deployment.
        - Coordinate with PD and FD during **traffic management or emergency situations**.
        - Follow **department protocols** for equipment, vehicles, and emergency response.
      </Accordion>
    </AccordionGroup>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Note>
  All departments must prioritize **realism, safety, and teamwork**. Always verify rank permissions before performing any action that affects roleplay scenarios.
</Note>
