## Discord Rules

To maintain a safe and fun environment in our Discord server, all members must follow these rules:

<Columns cols={1}>
  <Card
    title="1. Respect Others"
    icon="users"
  >
    Treat all members with respect. Harassment, hate speech, or bullying will not be tolerated.
  </Card>

  <Card
    title="2. No Spam"
    icon="ban"
  >
    Avoid spamming messages, images, or links in any channel.
  </Card>

  <Card
    title="3. Appropriate Content"
    icon="exclamation-triangle"
  >
    Do not post NSFW or inappropriate content anywhere on the server.
  </Card>

  <Card
    title="4. Follow Staff Instructions"
    icon="person-circle-check"
  >
    Always listen to moderators and admins. Their decisions are final in maintaining order.
  </Card>

  <Card
    title="5. Channel Usage"
    icon="hashtag"
  >
    Use the correct channels for your messages. Off-topic posts should go to general chat.
  </Card>
</Columns>

<Card
  title="Report Violations"
  icon="megaphone"
  href="/report-issues"
>
  If you see someone breaking the rules, report it to staff immediately.
</Card>
